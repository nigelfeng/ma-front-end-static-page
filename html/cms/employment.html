We offer the perfect blend of career advancement, compensation and recognition for a job well done in the exciting and growing music instrument industry.

Music & Arts opened for business in 1952 and now operates over 200+ stores throughout the country. Music & Arts locations are full-line stores featuring band & orchestra instruments, guitars & keyboards, printed music, music lessons, instrument rentals and musical accessories. Our mission is to bring the best in music to families, schools and individuals, while providing the highest level of customer service

Frequently Asked Questions: Instrument Rentals
Q:

Can I exchange later for a different instrument? What if my child outgrows the string instrument I am renting?

A:

We offer exchanges when your child is ready for a larger instrument. It is recommended for string instrument renters to check with your instructor to verify that the student is playing on the correct size instrument. If you are in need of another size string instrument, we offer this exchange at no additional charge.

For all of our instrument rentals, you may exchange for another instrument of equal or greater value throughout the life of the rental agreement. Some conditions apply. We do not generally exchange from one new instrument to another if you are exchanging to the same type of instrument. Should you wish to exchange your instrument, please contact Customer Service here Opens a New Window. for more details.

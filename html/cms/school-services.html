Meet Our Educational Representatives

How may we help you?

Our Educational Representatives work closely with you to handle instrument rentals but they also do so much more. Whether it’s with recruitment or classroom repairs, we’re with you every step of the way. We can provide you everything from one single reed to a classroom full of low brass and everything in between.

Many representatives are musicians themselves. Some are educators. They perform in bands, quartets, orchestras and ensembles. They teach. They understand instruments and music education. They support you.

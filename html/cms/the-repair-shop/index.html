When you need musical instrument repairs, come to Music & Arts. Whether it’s a minor adjustment or a major instrument repair, the Repair Shop at Music & Arts can help. Music & Arts offers high quality, affordable repairs on woodwinds, strings, brass, percussion, electronic instruments, and more.

Music & Arts has one of the largest musical instrument repair networks in the nation. We have 40 repair facilities that service each of our 200+ retail locations. Music & Arts is also an authorized service center for many of the world’s top instrument manufacturers.

Music & Arts Repair Shop services for musical instruments include dent removal, re-stringing, re-corking, structural repair, cleaning and polishing, readjustments, valve repair, replacing broken parts, and more. We also offer general instrument maintenance and water damage repair.

Not sure what kind of musical instrument repair you need? Don’t worry. At the Music & Arts Repair Shop, instrument repair estimates are always free. We’ll also show you the best way to take care of your instrument. Our friendly Repair Shop employees can share their expert knowledge for cleaning, storing and maintaining your instrument.

At the Music & Arts Repair Shop, we guarantee the highest quality service for musical instrument repairs. Our musical instrument repair technicians are seasoned professionals who have apprenticed with master instrument makers and trained at the best technical colleges. The Music & Arts Repair Center in Frederick, MD is also a training center for the next generation of musical instrument technicians.

COVID-19 Announcements

Reopening Stores

Across the country, in select states and counties, retail stores are being allowed to reopen. As we reopen stores in these markets, we do so with new safety measures. We remain committed to making sure our associates and customers stay healthy and safe. Please note: A face mask or covering is required to enter the store for your safety, and the safety of other customers and our staff. 

  • We have increased thorough and regular cleaning throughout the store
  • Our expert associates are happy to assist you from a safe distance wearing face masks at all times
  • Hand sanitizer is available throughout the store for your use, especially before handling merchandise
  • We are limiting the number of customers allowed in the store at a time to assure social distancing

When visiting our stores, we ask that you:

Please do not enter the store if you are sick or believe you may have been exposed to COVID-19

Wear a proper mask or face covering

Practice social distancing by staying six feet away from others

Request assistance before handling merchandise

If you prefer you can also order online and utilize our contactless curbside pickup at select store locations. To see if your store is open visit our store locator pages. 

Returns

To assist you and support your needs we have extended our return policy from 45 days to 180 days to return any items. 

Online Lessons

In store music lessons will remain temporarily closed but we are offering digital one-to-one lessons with our instructors via the Zoom platform. Our technology presents an opportunity to keep the Music & Arts lessons program available to students who want to learn or improve their skills, digitally.

While we understand that an in-person lesson is preferred, our instructors are utilizing Zoom technology to facilitate students’ weekly music lessons experience. For more information on our music lessons program, please visit our lessons page.

Rental Terms & Conditions
ADDITIONAL TERMS AND CONDITIONS YOU AGREE TO

1. LEASE TERM AND OPTIONS. At the end of your Initial Trial Period, you have four options: (1) continue renting the instrument by making another monthly rental payment on or before the end of the Initial Trial Period; (2) exercise your exchange option (see Items 2 and 3 below) for another instrument; (3) terminate this Agreement by returning the instrument to us in good condition, fair wear and tear excepted. Note: Rental payments are not contingent on practice or lesson schedules; (4) see Item 4 for instrument purchase opportunity. The Initial Trial Period and monthly payments will not be prorated or refunded. The Initial Trial Period cannot be extended or renewed. You cannot purchase the instrument you are renting (see Item 4 below). If you are uncertain as to how to return your instrument, please call 888-731-5396 for instructions. The initial trial period may or may not coincide with the school year.
2. RENTAL INSTRUMENT EXCHANGE POLICY. You may exchange to a student-level instrument of the same type, or from one student-level instrument to another of equal or greater value, and pay any additional rental charges which become due during this Agreement. This policy is only available if your account remains current throughout its term. See item 3 for fractional size string policy. Rental rates in effect at the time of exchange will apply. If you are already renting a new instrument and wish to exchange to another rental instrument, Music & Arts will exchange the instrument requested to a brand and condition of Music & Arts' choice.
3. FRACTIONAL SIZE STRING POLICY. You may exchange a student-level Violin, Viola, or Cello for a larger size student-level Violin, Viola, or Cello as the child grows. We will apply 100% of your rental payments (not including taxes, fees or Liability Damage Waiver) toward a larger instrument. If you exchange instruments without signing a new Agreement, you agree that the terms of this Agreement will apply to each instrument and exchange. Rental rates in effect at the time of exchange will apply.
4. INSTRUMENT PURCHASE. You cannot purchase the instrument you are renting. You can purchase another new or used student-level band or orchestra instrument, of equal or greater value, at any time after the original rental date. If purchasing a string or used band instrument, 100% of your rental payments made (not including taxes, fees or Liability Damage Waiver) will apply toward the current Music & Arts MSRP. If purchasing a new band instrument, 100% of your rental payments made in the first 24 months (not including taxes, fees or Liability Damage Waiver) will apply towards the current Music & Arts MSRP. After the rental credit has been applied, we may also offer a 30% discount on the remaining balance which is payable at time of purchase.  With approved credit you may finance the purchase through us. This option terminates upon return of the instrument. Once you have purchased the instrument, you have no further return or exchange options. Rental credits cannot be applied to On-Line Purchases through www.musicarts.com. Please direct instrument exchanges and purchases to your local store or school representative.
5. OFFICIAL AND OTHER FEES. Late Fee: $5.00 on payments not received within 10 days after the due date. Returned Check Fee: $25.00. Official Fees: None. These fees are subject to change if state law changes.
6. LIABILITY FOR LOSS OR DAMAGE. You are fully responsible for loss, theft, damage to or destruction of the instrument from all causes in excess of normal wear and tear, up to a maximum of the fair market value of the instrument. You can choose the optional Liability Damage Waiver (LDW) (Item 7 below) to cover some of your liability.
7. LIABILITY DAMAGE WAIVER (LDW) (optional but recommended). If you choose this option by paying the LDW fee and are not in default of this Agreement, you will not be liable for loss or damage to the instrument due to theft or fire. For theft, you must furnish us with a Police Report showing forced entry within 72 hours of the loss. The LDW does not cover mysterious disappearance from the school band or orchestra room or lockers. The LDW does not cover theft without proof of breaking and entering. We will not replace the instrument, and payments made prior to the date of loss or damage are not refundable or transferable. If you claim a loss under the LDW you agree that we are subrogated to all of your rights, claims, and remedies due to the loss of the instrument including but in no way limited to your rights or any recoveries under any insurance policies.
LDW covers certain repairs at no charge to you unless the damage is due to your willful act or negligence, in which case our standard repair rates apply. The LDW does not include damage to finishes, cosmetic damage to cases, case feet, string breakage (after the first 14 days of rental), reeds, drum sticks and mallets, heads, bow hair, rosin, brass and woodwind mouthpiece loss, neck straps, ligature loss and caps. Mouthpiece and ligature, included with instrument at time of rental, are covered upon presentation of the broken part. ALL REPAIRS MUST BE DONE BY A MUSIC & ARTS APPROVED TECHNICIAN.
You can choose the LDW when this Agreement is initiated. You cannot add it later. The LDW may not be available on all types of instruments.
8. DEFAULT. If you fail to make a rental payment within 10 days of the due date, or fail to observe any other term in this Agreement, you are in default and this Agreement terminates. If you default, and after we give you any notice required by law, we are entitled to all legal remedies, including repossession and our damages. If you default, you must pay us for all of our reasonable costs of collection which may include some or all of the following: a $50 repossession fee, a 30% third party debt collection fee, late invoice fees and a $15 third party administrative collection fee plus any other court costs and/or legal fees, as permitted by law. These amounts are in addition to the underlying balance due. Upon default, any rental credit earned will be void, and you are liable for amounts due us and immediate return of the instrument. If you are in default, Music & Arts, a Division of Guitar Center Stores, Inc., reserves the right to report your credit and payment history to any of the credit reporting agencies at our option.
9. MAINTENANCE AND REPAIR. If you do not choose the LDW option, you are responsible for maintaining the instrument in good working condition during this Agreement. Our standard repair rates apply if the instrument is returned in a damaged condition or otherwise needs repairs up to a maximum charge equal to the fair market value of the instrument.
10. TERMINATION. You can terminate this Agreement at any time, without penalty, by returning the instrument to us in good condition, fair wear and tear excepted. All original issue accessories such as strings, mouthpieces, drum pads, sticks, instrument parts, or bows must be returned with the instrument, or you must pay for them at the time of return. DO NOT LEAVE THE INSTRUMENT AT SCHOOL WITHOUT PROPER ADVANCE AUTHORIZATION FROM US.

You must inform us in writing of any address changes. All rental payments are due in advance. There are no refunds on rental payments. Note: You are responsible for your monthly payments even if you do not receive a bill. Errors in the billing process and with the postal service may occur. You authorize us to receive updated credit card information including a new number or expiration date from the credit card issuer. You can cancel this authorization at any time by notifying us. You authorize us to charge any active card on file for any past due payments.

You must not sell, pawn, dispose of or pledge the instrument as security while this Agreement is in effect. You remain liable under this Agreement unless you have received a return receipt from us. Your school is not a party to this transaction.

15. SECURITY DEPOSIT. If you pay a security deposit, we can use it on any outstanding balance under this Agreement when this Agreement terminates. Upon satisfactory return of the instrument, we will refund the deposit to you within 4 weeks of the actual return date, less any amounts you owe us. Unless required by law, we do not keep your security deposit in a separate account, and it does not bear interest. Past due payments will not be offset against the security deposit.

By signing this Agreement, you accept all liability for making timely rental payments under this Agreement as well as for the condition of the instrument until it has been returned to us. If you want to transfer billing responsibility to another party, you must get such a change cleared in writing through Music & Arts.
